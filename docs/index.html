Sustainability

Hard facts:
- Animal agriculture is the leading cause of climate change
- The industry occupies a third of all arable land (If everyone adapted a vegan diet by 2050, we could sequester 772 GtCO2 which is the equivalent of 21 years of our current fossil fuel emissions)
- The average person only gets 18% of calories from animal products
- Water usage: A vegan uses on average a million liters less water than an omnivore. That is an equivolant to 30 years of 10 minute long daily showers.
- Methane has an instantenous GWP 124 times greater than that of CO2.
- Oceans store heat so immediate heating matters
- Coral reefs are vital for carbon sequestration, but they are dying due to overfishing and increased warmth. Some have already started "bleaching" due to the increased temperature.
- Honey bees are in direct competition for food with wild bees, which leads to a decrease of 48% of wild bee abundance around bee hive presence. Wild bees are also much better pollinators than honey bees, but most importantly we need biodiversity
